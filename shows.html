Natural Selections

Works by Joan Wiener. A photographic exploration of how and why things are the way they are. With most images taken via electron and light microscopes, this series of giclee prints and transparent photo-transfers takes a closer look at the form and function of everyday objects.

A biological and artistic collaboration between the Botanic Garden of Smith College, the Smith College Microscopy and Imaging Facility, and local artist Joan Wiener.
